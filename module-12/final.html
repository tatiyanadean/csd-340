Musical Experience

Performing

I first sang in church, but I don't consider those performances. In my mind, my first performance was at a middle school poetry slam where I sung a "Fried Chicken" remix of T-Pain's "Shawty" (of which footage I am determined to hunt down).

During my freshmen year at University of Louisville, I sung at campus event and open mics around the city, sometimes traveling out of town to compete.

After Louisville, I moved to Atlanta where I performed anywhere from 1-3 times a week at various showcases, opening for artists and singing/rapping in competitions. Above is a flyer of one of my last (and favorite) performances in Atlanta before moving back to Kentucky (Fun Fact: This was my 1st paid performance).

Songwriting

I write all of my own songs, which is something I didn't understand the value of until I learned that many singers can't say the same.

Sometimes a label will require a singer to record or perform someone else's lyrics, but I am blessed to be an independent artist so I have the final and only say in what I do with my voice.

Above are the songs I've written, recorded, and released on Apple Music (and other platforms) since 2020.

Recording

In 2021, I finally was able to invest in my own home studio equipment. An extremely huge blessing, having my own studio is allowing me to record songs without having to pay for studio time (which quickly gets expensive when being charged hourly).
